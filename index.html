Try 07 Kit

An open source client for OldSchool RuneScape.


We've decided to go open source to allow for greater collaboration with the community. Users will be able to improve existing plugins and build their own, allowing for more customization and more frequent updates. The added benefit is that you can inspect the source code yourself, so you know it's safe!

Target information

Shows information about the target you're attacking, such as remaining health (% for players, exact for NPCs).

Skill information

Skill overlays show you information relevant to the skill you're training, such as: actions completed, actions to level, profit and highlights related NPCs and objects in-game.

Experience tracker

Our XP tracker orbs show you how much XP you've gained during your game session, how much XP is left until you hit the next level and how long we think it will take you to get there.

Inventory markers

Display a coloured overlay on top of items in your inventory, so you can see what's what in the blink of an eye. It really helps with things like enchanting jewellery.

Loot markers

Loot markers allow you to highlight interesting items on the screen. You can highlight items based on name and/or price.

Npc markers

Highlights NPCs of interest on the screen and the mini-map. You can mark/un-mark NPCs simply by right-clicking them in-game.

And lots more...

We've got a lot more to offer when it comes to overlays, and the list is always expanding. We recommend you take us up on our 1-week trial offer and see for yourself.

World switcher

Our world switcher located in the sidebar lets you search for worlds and shows you the type, number of players, country, ping, and the worlds designated "activity". Simply hop to a world by double-clicking it.

Hi-score lookup

Our hi-score lookup can also be found in the sidebar, and let's you quickly look up the hi-scores for a specific player. You can even right-click players in-game and use the "Hiscore" menu option to look them up quickly.

Grand exchange

Quickly see the status of all your outstanding Grand Exchange offers. We'll even send you a notification if any of them change!

Experience tracker

Yep, we also have an experience tracker in the sidebar to complement the overlay. It shows the experience gains for your entire session.

Social stream

We'll keep you updated on the latest social buzz, with a list of interesting Twitch streams and social media activity.

Notes

For those times when you need to quickly write something down before you forget.

Drag-and-drop overlays

We made all of our box overlays draggable, so you can move them to a place where they won't get in your way.

Useful notifications

Most of the features in our client are capable of generating notifications, but here are some notable ones: idle notification, combat notification (being attacked while client isn't in focus), mentions (when someone mentions you in-game), trade (when someone sends you a trade request), health/prayer low.
